Details
Chote Nawab is Casual Dinning Restuarant Which Serves North Indian and Chinese Cuisine along with amazing Desserts, This Outlet is Situated at 1, Opposite Inlaks Hospital, Chembur Camp, Chembur, Mumbai, This Place has Upstairs Seatings, with Decent Ambience, Decors and Interiors. We Visited This Place Chote Nawab on Weekend To have Lunch With My Friends, In Which We Mocktails like Virgin Mojito, Virgin Pina Colada and Watermelon Punch, along with Starters/Appetizers like Kebab Platter, Chicken Tikka and Butter Chicken with associated Indian Breads and caramel Custard in Desserts Located in the busy streets of Chembur, the place is a little tedious to find. The place is under renovation and is getting bigger and better. The ambiance is like the name itself, trys to give the Nawabi feels. The service was good and responsive.Time to check their menu out:Mocktails: Appletini(apple and lemon based) and Electrifying(watermelon based). Both the drinks were good.Paneer 65: Tangy,delicious and lips smacking. Tender cubes of paneer given the perfect desi chinese twist.Chicken Reshmi Tikka: Succulent and tender pieces of chicken marinated in a creamy mixture.Chicken Crispy: The only thing which let us down. It was chewy more than crispy, could have been better.Veg Korma with Butter Naan: The curry was really amazing, it had the perfect amount of spice and the pieces were also prepared perfectly.Caramel Custard: Loved the custard as it was not too sweet and the flavors were well balanced.Must try: Electrifying, Paneer 65, Veg Korma.Food: 4.5/5Service: 4/5Ambiance: 4/5
