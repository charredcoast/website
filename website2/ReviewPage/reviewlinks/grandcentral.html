One of Chemburâ€™s oldest restaurants, Grand Central in Chembur East serves both vegetarian and non-vegetarian Indian food. Located near Chembur Railway Station, one can easily commute to this restaurant without any hassles. Established in 1974, this multi-cuisine restaurant serves buffets and an a la carte menu. They specialize in serving delicious North Indian, Chinese, Asian, Seafood and Biryani meals. Open from 11:00 am - 03:30 pm and 06:00 pm - 11:30 pm, one can easily dine as per their convenience. Known for traditional Indian food offerings they also serve Mexican food. You also have to watch out for the Chinese Food Festival that is held in this restaurant from time to time. Undoubtedly it is one of the best restaurants in Chembur East Mumbai. Grand Central in Chembur East, boasts of some good Mughlai fare, especially non vegetarian chicken dishes. The most-sought-after dishes are the Chicken Lollipops, Butter Chicken, Peeli Mirch ka Paneer Tikka, Egg Biryani, Paneer in Black Bean Sauce, Kolhapuri Chicken and lots more. One cannot go home without trying their lovely selection of wines and finger licking kebab platters. They also provide excellent party catering services. Their calm and quiet ambience makes for a pleasant meal. They have basic seating that is also comfortable. The service is top notch and has been for years. The staff is courteous and prompt. The atmosphere is the best in Chembur for a family gathering with ghazals in the background. You can also book a table at this popular restaurant to have a memorable dining experience with your family and friends.
