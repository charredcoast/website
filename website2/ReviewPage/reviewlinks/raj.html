About

Description
Whereabouts and Overview: A well crafted venue, Raj Restaurant & Bar at Chembur East, Mumbai is home to simply finger-licking food. Attracting food lovers, the restaurant's location at along Dr CG Road at Chembur East makes commuting a hassle-free task for visitors. With a number of conveyance options at one's disposal, guests need not fret about finding their way around. In addition to the food, what keeps patrons coming to the restaurant is the overall dining experience that is a combination of exemplary service and hospitality, an appealing ambiance and polite staff. Not only does the staff provide quick service, but also renders assistance while an order is being placed by making valuable suggestions. Cuisine and Ambiance: On a visit to Raj Restaurant & Bar at Chembur East, guests can expect to be spoiled for choice. Popular for an array of North Indian and Mughlai preparations, the restaurant is also home to tempting Chinese food. The menu has a number of categories providing an array of options for one to choose from. Some of the most ordered dishes include Mushroom Chilly, Paneer Teacher, Veg Crispy, Chicken Marco, Fish Chilly, Aloo Chaat, Chicken Cream, Veg Handi, Punjabi Dahi Kadi, Chana Masala, Chicken Tikka Masala, Mutton Dahiwala, Veg Dum Biryani, Chicken Tikka Biryani and Mutton Biryani. Offering a cozy and delightful atmosphere, the restaurant premises have been ably decorated. As the soothing hues used in the interiors and décor impart a very pleasant feel to the dining area, the seating accommodation offers one complete comfort so that a meal can be enjoyed. The simplistic décor adds a subtle touch of elegance to the overall dining space. The light color combination is the key factor that makes the restaurant a pleasant space. Services Available and Highlights: In addition to a delightful food menu, Raj Restaurant & Bar at Chembur East also has a separate drinks menu with a wide collection of spirits. It welcomes guests from 12 pm to 1 am. The air-conditioned ambiance offers diners much relief from the weather outside and makes for a relaxing atmosphere. Patrons can avail the home delivery service offered by the rest by clicking on the 'Order Online' tab and placing an order effortlessly. Undoubtedly it is one of the Best Restaurants in Chembur East, Mumbai. Please scroll up to view the address and contact details of Raj Restaurant & Bar at Chembur East, Mumbai.
